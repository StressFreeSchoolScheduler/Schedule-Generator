Carleton Schedule Generator

An intelligent tool that builds conflict‑free, optimized course schedules in seconds.

The Problem

Carleton students spend hours manually building schedules, checking conflicts, and trying to optimize their week. Advisors receive countless emails about timetable issues, and registration stress peaks every semester.

The Solution

The Carleton Schedule Generator automatically creates every valid schedule based on a student's courses and preferences. Students can choose:

  • Preferred class times
  • Day off / night off
  • Blocked time ranges
  • Compact or balanced week structure

Schedules are generated instantly and displayed in a clean, visual timetable.
